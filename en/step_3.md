# Step 2: Your ChatBot

+ Before you start making your chatbot, you need to decide on its personality. __Think about__:

	+ What is their name?
	+ Where do they live?
	+ Are they happy? serious? funny? shy? friendly?

+ Open a new empty Scratch project.
